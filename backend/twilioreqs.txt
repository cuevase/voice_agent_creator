What businesses need: 
If individual: 
    - Proof of Identity with government issued id or passport
    - Proof of local adress with: government issued id showing adress, recent utility bill, or recent government communication. 

If Business: 
    - Proof of identity with business registration:
        - Constancia de Situacion Fiscal con RFC y razon social
        - Acta Constitutiva inscrita en el Registro Publico de Comercio
        - Cedula de Identificacion Fiscal del SAT
    - Business Adress with either: Business registration showing local adress, recent utility bill, or recent government communication
        - COnstancia de Situacion Fiscal con tu nombre, RFC y regimen
